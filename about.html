About

The Canadian Engineering Leadership Conference (CELC) is an annual, bilingual, student-run event that functions as the annual general meeting of the Canadian Federation of Engineering Students (CFES) and also a national platform for students to collaborate with, and learn from, industry partners. Each year, CELC brings together 200 delegates from over 40 engineering schools across Canada, offering a unique opportunity to engage with student leaders from every institution and discipline within the engineering community.
